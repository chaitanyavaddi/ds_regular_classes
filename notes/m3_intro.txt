Business Thinking + Understanding a dataset

Definition data:

Data is raw information collected from customers, 
products, transactions, or any operations

Examples:
* Customer names
* Sales amount
* Product categories
* Shipping dates
* Location (city, state..)

Definition Dataset:

A dataset is a structured collection of data stored in 
rows and columns usually in CSV / Excel format. 

Examples:
* Rows  = orders
* Columns = Sales, profit, category, etc..
Customer name	Sale amount	Sale date
John	400	03-05-2025

Definition: Business Problem

A Challenge or a goal a company wants to solve using data

Examples:
* “Which products are not profitable?”
* ‘Which city buys the most items?”
* ‘Why is profit going down?”


Definition: KPI (Key Performance Indicator)

A measurable value that shows how well the business is performing

Examples:
* Revenue
* Profit
* Customer retention
* Monthly sales
* Average order value
