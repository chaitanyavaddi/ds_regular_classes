How to write EDA Insights?

- Observation    -> What you see in the chart?
- Reason         -> Why this happened?
- Impact         -> Why it matters?
- Recommendation -> What CEO should do?


Eg. 
Observation: technology category is generating higest sales
Reason: High ticket items like laptops, printers
Impact: high revenue, stable profit
Recommendation: Increase promotions in Technology category
